DISTRICT COLLECTOR OFFICE

The Collector Office is the main administrative office in a district. It is headed by the District Collector, who is one of the top government officials responsible for the overall development and smooth running of the district.It is the link between the government and the people.Helps the public with important services like certificates (community, income, birth, death, etc.), land documents, and grievance redressal.Acts as the decision-making hub for district-level planning and development.
